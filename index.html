College & Career Center Animated GIF

This animated GIF was created for our school's College & Career Center to promote their services. Using Adobe Photoshop's timeline feature, I designed a sequence that highlights key resources available to students. The animation begins with our school mascot guiding viewers through different college planning tools, followed by statistics about our graduates' success. I paid special attention to smooth transitions between scenes and consistent branding colors throughout. The project taught me valuable lessons about timing in animations and creating engaging content for educational purposes.

Colt Brew Logos

These logo designs were created for a hypothetical school coffee shop called "Colt Brew" (our mascot is the Colts). I developed three distinct variations exploring different styles: a classic badge, a modern minimalist mark, and an illustrative approach. Each version maintains brand consistency while offering unique visual appeal. The project involved extensive sketching, vector refinement in Adobe Illustrator, and testing at various sizes. The final logos work effectively in both color and black-and-white applications, demonstrating my growing understanding of scalable vector design.

Animated Self-Portrait

This animated self-portrait combines digital illustration with motion graphics to create a personal branding piece. Using frame-by-frame animation techniques, I brought my illustrated avatar to life with subtle movements and expressions. The project challenged me to consider how personality can be conveyed through animation timing and motion paths. I experimented with different easing techniques to make the movements feel natural. The color palette was carefully selected to represent my artistic style while maintaining visual harmony throughout the animation sequence.

Library Logo Design

Created for our school library's rebranding initiative, this logo combines literary elements with our school identity. The open book forms the shape of our mascot's horseshoe, symbolizing both learning and school pride. I went through multiple iterations, testing different typography treatments and icon combinations. The final design works effectively at small sizes for library cards and large formats for wall graphics. This project deepened my understanding of symbolic representation in logo design and the importance of creating versatile branding assets.

Digital Illustration Series

This series of digital illustrations explores different artistic styles and techniques. Using Adobe Photoshop and a graphics tablet, I experimented with brush settings, layer blending modes, and color theory principles. Each piece in the series demonstrates my progression in digital painting skills, from basic shading to more advanced lighting effects. The project helped me develop my personal artistic voice while learning to adapt to different visual styles. Particular focus was given to creating depth and texture in digital media.

Audio Composition

This original audio composition was created using digital audio workstation software. The piece combines synthesized elements with recorded sounds to create an atmospheric musical work. I explored concepts of rhythm, melody, and sound design, layering different tracks to build complexity. The project involved learning about equalization, compression, and spatial effects to create a polished final product. This foray into audio production complemented my visual work, helping me understand the relationship between sound and image in multimedia projects.

Digital Painting: DDT

This digital painting explores the theme of environmental impact through surreal imagery. Created entirely in Photoshop, the piece demonstrates advanced brush techniques and photo manipulation skills. I focused on creating realistic textures and lighting effects to sell the impossible scenario. The composition guides the viewer's eye through careful arrangement of elements and value contrast. This project pushed my technical abilities while allowing creative expression of an important message about ecological responsibility.

Typography Poster Series

This typography-focused poster series explores the relationship between letterforms and visual communication. Each poster in the series experiments with different type treatments, hierarchy, and composition principles. I researched historical type classifications and contemporary trends to inform my designs. The project challenged me to communicate messages purely through typographic arrangement, without relying on illustrative elements. Attention was given to legibility at various viewing distances and the emotional impact of different typeface selections.
